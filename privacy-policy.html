Privacy Policy

How platform request information may be handled.

This policy applies to information submitted through this website and related provider-matching request forms.

Last updated: 2026 Company: Skylin Provider Matching LLC

1. Platform role

Skylin is an independent provider-matching platform for window-related service categories. Skylin does not perform window installation, replacement, repair, inspection, energy-efficiency work, or any other window services directly.

Skylin is an independent provider-matching platform. Skylin does not perform window installation, replacement, repair, inspection, or energy-efficiency work directly. Provider availability, qualifications, quotes, timelines, warranties, and service terms must be reviewed directly with each independent provider.

2. Information you may provide

When you use the website, contact form, phone links, email links, or provider-matching request modules, you may choose to provide information such as:

  • Name and contact details.
  • Email address and phone number.
  • ZIP code, city, state, or service area information.
  • Window service category, such as installation, replacement, repair, or energy-efficient windows.
  • Project notes, timing preferences, property context, and request details.
  • Information you submit voluntarily in form fields, messages, calls, or emails.

3. How information may be used

Information submitted through this website may be used to:

  • Respond to your request or inquiry.
  • Organize window project details for provider-matching purposes.
  • Help connect you with independent provider options where available.
  • Communicate by phone, email, text, or other contact methods where permitted.
  • Improve website usability, request flow, form clarity, and platform operations.
  • Maintain records related to website submissions and platform requests.

4. Sharing with independent providers

To help with provider matching, information you submit may be shared with independent local providers, service partners, or platform support vendors. These parties are independent from Skylin and are responsible for their own services, communications, quotes, availability, credentials, warranties, and work.

Homeowners should verify licensing, insurance, provider credentials, quote details, timelines, warranty terms, and service terms directly with any provider before moving forward.

5. Cookies, analytics, and local storage

This website may use cookies, local storage, analytics tools, or similar technologies to support basic website functionality, remember policy preferences, understand site usage, and improve user experience.

The cookie/policy banner choice may be stored in local storage so the website can remember whether you accepted or declined the banner.

For more detail, review the Cookie Policy.

6. Communications

By submitting a request, you may be contacted about your inquiry using the contact details you provide. Communications may relate to provider matching, project details, follow-up questions, service category clarification, or related platform support.

Communication frequency, method, and availability may vary depending on the request, service area, and provider availability.

7. Data accuracy and homeowner responsibility

You are responsible for providing accurate request information. Skylin may rely on the details you submit to organize your request and assist with provider matching.

Skylin does not independently verify every provider statement, quote, license, insurance policy, warranty term, timeline, or service claim. You should review all important details directly with the provider.

8. Data security

Skylin uses reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational practices intended to protect submitted information. However, no website, transmission method, storage system, or online process can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

9. Third-party websites and providers

This website may link to third-party pages, independent provider websites, legal pages, or external resources. Skylin is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, communications, services, or policies of third parties.

10. Children’s privacy

This website is intended for use by adults seeking information about window provider matching. It is not intended for children.

11. Changes to this policy

Skylin may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates may be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.
