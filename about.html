Platform role

What does — and does not do.

The distinction matters. is designed for provider matching and request organization, not direct window service work.

Skylin does
  • Help homeowners compare local window provider options.
  • Organize project requests by category and ZIP code.
  • Connect users with independent window companies.
  • Encourage direct verification of licensing, insurance, quotes, timelines, and warranty terms.
Skylin does not
  • Install windows directly.
  • Replace windows directly.
  • Repair or inspect windows directly.
  • Employ window technicians, installers, or crews.
  • Guarantee provider work, availability, pricing, or timelines.

Homeowner verification

Always verify details directly before hiring.

Providers are independent. Before selecting any provider, homeowners should review the details that matter for their specific window project.

  • Licensing and insurance requirements
  • Quote scope, exclusions, and payment terms
  • Window materials, glass packages, and product documentation
  • Timeline, access needs, cleanup, and warranty terms
