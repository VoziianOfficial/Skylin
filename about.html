Does / Does Not
Platform boundaries

What Skylin does — and what it does not do.

The platform language must stay clear: Skylin is not a window installer, replacement company, repair crew, inspection team, or direct contractor.

Skylin does

  • Help homeowners compare independent local window provider options.
  • Organize request details by service category and project context.
  • Help connect users with provider options where available.
  • Encourage direct verification of licensing, insurance, quotes, timelines, and warranty terms.

Skylin does not

  • Install, replace, repair, inspect, or service windows directly.
  • Employ window technicians, installers, repair crews, or contractors.
  • Guarantee provider availability, pricing, quote accuracy, timelines, or project outcomes.
  • Warrant or guarantee work performed by independent providers.
Focused categories

A narrower service model makes comparison easier.
