2. What cookies are

Cookies are small files or browser-based storage tools that may be placed on your device when you visit a website. Similar technologies may include local storage, session storage, pixels, tags, scripts, or analytics identifiers.

3. Types of technologies this website may use

This website may use the following types of cookies or similar technologies:

  • Essential tools: Used to support core site functionality, form behavior, accessibility, navigation, or security.
  • Preference tools: Used to remember choices such as cookie banner accept or decline status.
  • Analytics tools: Used to understand general website usage, page activity, traffic patterns, and user experience issues.
  • Performance tools: Used to improve loading, layout behavior, and interaction quality.
  • Marketing or referral tools: May be used to understand how visitors reach the site or interact with provider-matching requests.

4. Local storage for policy banner choice

The website may store your policy banner choice in local storage so the banner does not repeatedly appear after you accept or decline it.

The configured storage key may be controlled through the global website config file. Clearing browser data may reset this preference.

5. Why these tools may be used

Cookies, local storage, and similar technologies may be used to:

  • Remember privacy or cookie banner choices.
  • Support website navigation and interactive elements.
  • Improve form usability and request-flow clarity.
  • Understand which pages are visited and how users interact with the website.
  • Improve performance, accessibility, and layout behavior.
  • Support provider-matching platform operations.

6. Third-party tools

The website may use third-party tools for analytics, performance, advertising measurement, communication, or platform support. These third parties may set or read cookies, pixels, scripts, or similar technologies according to their own policies.

Skylin is not responsible for the privacy or cookie practices of independent third-party websites, providers, vendors, or external services.

7. Managing cookies

You can often manage cookies through your browser settings. Browser controls may allow you to block cookies, delete stored data, clear local storage, or limit tracking technologies.

Blocking some cookies or storage tools may affect website functionality, including policy banner preferences, forms, analytics, or interactive features.

8. Provider-matching request information

Cookie tools are separate from the information you voluntarily submit through forms, calls, emails, or request modules. Request information may be used to respond to your inquiry, organize window project details, and help connect you with independent provider options where available.

For more information about submitted data, review the Privacy Policy.

9. No direct window service claim

Use of cookies, analytics, or platform tools does not change the role of Skylin. Skylin is not a window contractor and does not perform window work directly.
